## Why This Source Matters

OpenStax Biology 2e is the deeper textbook spine for this course. It covers a two-semester introductory biology sequence for science majors, so it extends beyond the review target while still giving reliable explanations for the fundamentals.

## Translation Into Local Lessons

The local Biology lessons use OpenStax as a depth reserve. When a topic deserves more than a review definition, the lesson should lean on OpenStax-style mechanisms:

- molecules have shapes, charges, and interactions,
- membranes are dynamic barriers, not plastic bags,
- energy transformations obey thermodynamics,
- inheritance is chromosome behavior plus probability,
- gene expression is regulated information flow,
- evolution changes populations, not individual organisms,
- ecology studies systems with feedback and tradeoffs.
