## How To Use Practice Problems
- For short-answer problems: answer in 1 to 3 lines with the exact quantity and rate.
- For proof problems: target 6 to 10 lines, not homework-length derivations.
- For KKT problems: solve, then verify all KKT conditions explicitly.

## What To Memorize Before Drills
- Core theorem statements and assumptions.
- Which quantities converge in each method.
- Standard proof ingredients for SGD, duality, KKT, SDP, and Newton.
- High-frequency traps from prior quiz/exam performance.

## Best Drill Order
- SGD / proximal gradient precision.
- Duality and KKT setup.
- SDP weak duality identity.
- Newton descent direction.
- Momentum / adaptive method distinctions.
