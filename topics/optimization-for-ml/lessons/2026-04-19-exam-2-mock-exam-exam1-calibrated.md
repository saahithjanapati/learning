# Exam 2 Mock Exam - Exam 1 Calibrated

This mock is designed to match the *difficulty profile* of the real `Exam 1` more closely than the earlier Exam 2 mock.

The goal is not to make the questions longer. The goal is to make them more like the actual exam:

- more `sufficiency vs necessity` traps
- more `quantity / sign / domain` traps
- fewer obvious “one false statement, everything else true” patterns
- short answers that require the right setup, not just theorem recall
