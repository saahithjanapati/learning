## Problem 2.1

Suppose the directed graph is
$$
X_1 \to X_2,\qquad X_1 \to X_3,\qquad X_3 \to X_4.
$$

Write the Bayesian-network factorization of
$$
p(x_1,x_2,x_3,x_4).
$$

## Problem 2.2

Suppose the undirected graph has edges
$$
(1,2),\ (1,3),\ (2,3).
$$

Write a valid clique-based factorization of the joint distribution, and explain why a normalizing constant is needed.

## Problem 2.3

Explain the difference between:

1. a conditional probability table in a directed model
2. a potential function in an undirected model

Your answer should mention normalization.

## Problem 2.4

In one short paragraph, explain what the partition function
$$
Z
$$
is doing in an undirected model and why it often becomes a computational bottleneck.
