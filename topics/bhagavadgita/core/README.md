# Bhagavad Gita / Core

Path: `topics/bhagavadgita/core`

## Scope

Beginner-friendly reading path through the Bhagavad Gita, organized around what happens in the dialogue, what each teaching means, and how major interpretations fit together.

The subject is split into four sections:

1. **Orientation and setting**: how to read the Gita, the Mahabharata context, and the core vocabulary.
2. **Action and discipline**: Arjuna's crisis, self-knowledge, karma yoga, renunciation, and meditation.
3. **Devotion and divine vision**: Krishna's divine reality, remembrance, bhakti, divine manifestations, and the universal form.
4. **Metaphysics and final synthesis**: body and self, the gunas, the cosmic tree, character, faith, and Krishna's final teaching.
